Use [[Encyclopedia/Objects/Materials/Mage-glass\|Mage-glass]] to summon temporary versions of some [[Game Rules/Gear/Weapon list\|weapons]]. Valid weapons are spears, arrows, javelins, daggers, and darts. The summoned weapons are [[Game Rules/Gear/Weapon keywords\|fragile]].
